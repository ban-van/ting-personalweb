About Me

I’m Ivan Ting, a freelance photographer who believes that creativity is sparked by curiosity. I find inspiration in quiet moments, wild ideas, and deep storytelling. From capturing candid photographs to crafting emotionally charged visuals—I turn ideas into experiences.

What Can I Offer

I specialize in capturing authentic moments through creative shots. My approach is rooted in curiosity and artistic vision. I transform these moments into striking visual narratives that endure and resonate.

I create meaningful films and dynamic video content. My work is guided by storytelling and the power of visuals. The result is cinematic pieces that bring stories and concepts to life with style.

I design visually compelling posters that communicate messages with clarity. Each poster is crafted with artistic direction and strategy to connect with the intended audience.
